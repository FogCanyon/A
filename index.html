About Us

We are a small company based in Tartu, consisting of 3 automation engineers and a part-time full stack programmer. Our experience is mainly in the Scandinavian and Baltic countries, with a strong focus on the wood industry, which makes up 90% of our work.

Our Team

My Background:

Our Clients

Our clients primarily include:

  • Sawmills in Estonia and Latvia (Nordwood sawmills, Stora Enso sawmills, Vara sawmill). We are actively involved in 6-7 sawmills.
  • Wood planing companies (Raitwood, Puidukoda).
  • Machine builders (local companies such as ValonKone, Rosens, etc).
  • Subcontracting projects in the energy industry (CGI, Diotech).
